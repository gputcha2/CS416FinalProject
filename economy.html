What is the relationship between a country's economy and happiness?

Home

Here, we will explore the relationship between some key economic indicators & how happy the citizen of that country is.

GDP and Income Group are some of the most widely used indicators of a country's economic health. We will try to analyze the relationship between these factors and the happiness scores of countries.

Here once again, we can draw a trend line to observe the relationship between these two measures. It becomes very clear that there is a positive correlation between GDP and a country's happiness score.

For countries with a greater GDP, we observe higher happiness scores.

On the bar chart, we can also observe the average happiness scores for the four different income groups. Clearly, higher income countries have higher happiness scores.

Hover your mouse pointer over any of the points in the scatterplot to view more information on the country.
