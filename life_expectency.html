What is the relationship between life expectency and happiness?

Home

Here, we will explore the relationship between average life expectency & how happy the citizen of that country is.

Life expectency at birth is one of the most frequently used health status indicators for a country. We can try to understand the impact general health status has on the happiness of a country's inhabitants.

Here once again, we can draw a trend line to observe the relationship between these two measures. Once again, it becomes very clear that there is a clear correlation between life expectency and a country's happiness score.

For countries with a greater life expectency, we observe higher happiness scores.
